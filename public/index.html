What is this workshop all about

Have you been amazed by websites like Facebook, Twitter and Gmail? You wonder how they are build? How a comment by your friend pops up on your wall soo fast?

We teach you everything you need to know about web development. Mentor you on best practices as you work together to build your web application from the concept.

Studying C/C++ since 12th grade and yet you can't code? Time to get your hands dirty! Code with the world's easiest yet most powerful programming language. JavaScript

The best way to learn something is when you teach yourself. No teaching you the theory like any other institute course, pure learning through practical experience. We guide you to hack your way out when you get stuck on those challenging problems.
